## ðŸ” Key Insights

### High-Energy Feedstocks (Best for Combustion/Pyrolysis)
- All grain straws and stovers (except beans/peas)
- All nut prunings (almonds, walnuts, pistachios, pecans)
- Oilseed crops (sunflower, safflower, soybeans)

### High-Moisture Feedstocks (Best for Anaerobic Digestion)
- All vegetables (24 crops)
- Legumes (beans, peas)
- Sugar beets
- Melon vines

### Year-Round Available
- Carrots
- Lettuce
- Green onions
- Spices & herbs
- Unspecified vegetables

### Winter Pruning Season (Jan-Feb)
- Most orchard and vineyard crops
- Stone fruits, pome fruits, citrus
- Nuts
- Grapes
